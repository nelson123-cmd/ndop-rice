Our Projects & Achievements

Transforming rice production in Cameroon through innovative projects

Current Projects

Buhlami Enterprise is engaged in several transformative projects that are revolutionizing rice production in the Ndop plain and beyond. These initiatives combine agricultural development with social impact to create sustainable change.

Farm Expansion Initiative

Our ongoing expansion project aims to increase cultivated land from 50 to 200 hectares over the next three years. This includes land preparation, irrigation infrastructure, and mechanization to boost productivity.

  • Status: Phase 1 completed (50-100 hectares)
  • Investment: $250,000
  • Impact: Will create 150+ new jobs
Learn More

Modern Processing Unit

Construction of a state-of-the-art rice processing facility with cleaning, hulling, polishing, and packaging capabilities. This will enable us to process 5 tonnes per hour with minimal breakage and maximum quality.

  • Status: Equipment procurement phase
  • Investment: $500,000
  • Impact: Will add value to local production
Learn More

Educational Scholarship Program

Our commitment to community development includes sponsoring bright students from farming families to pursue agricultural studies and other disciplines at secondary and tertiary levels.

  • Status: Ongoing
  • Investment: $20,000 annually
  • Impact: 15 students currently sponsored
Learn More

Key Achievements

Certified Quality

Received national certification for our rice quality meeting Cameroonian standards (NC 001:2019).

Employment Created

Provided stable employment for over 100 local community members with fair wages.

Production Growth

Increased yield from 2.5 tonnes/hectare to 4.8 tonnes/hectare through improved practices.

Water Management

Implemented sustainable irrigation system serving our farm and neighboring fields.

Education Support

Sponsored 15 students through our scholarship program since inception.

Mechanization

Introduced mechanized farming equipment reducing labor intensity by 40%.
