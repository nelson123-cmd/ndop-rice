About Buhlami Enterprise

Founded in 2018, Buhlami Enterprise has grown from a small family rice farm to one of the most promising agricultural ventures in the North West Region of Cameroon. Located in the fertile plains of Ndop, we specialize in the cultivation, processing, and distribution of premium quality rice.

Our mission is to achieve food security, create employment opportunities, and contribute to the economic development of Cameroon through sustainable rice production. We combine traditional farming knowledge with modern agricultural techniques to produce rice that meets international standards.

Why Choose Buhlami Rice?

Natural Irrigation

Our farms benefit from the natural water systems of the Ndop plain, ensuring optimal growth conditions without excessive artificial irrigation.

Sustainable Practices

We employ eco-friendly farming methods that preserve soil fertility and protect the local ecosystem.

Quality Processing

Our state-of-the-art processing unit ensures clean, well-milled rice with minimal breakage and maximum nutritional retention.

Community Focus

We reinvest in local communities through employment, education initiatives, and infrastructure development.
