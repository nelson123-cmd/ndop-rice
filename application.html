Partnership & Investment Opportunities

Join us in revolutionizing rice production in Cameroon

Why Partner With Buhlami Enterprise?

Buhlami Enterprise offers unique investment opportunities in Cameroon's growing agricultural sector. With increasing demand for locally produced rice and government support for agricultural development, now is the ideal time to invest in our expansion plans.

We're seeking partners who share our vision of sustainable agricultural development and community empowerment. Whether you're interested in direct investment, equipment supply, technical partnership, or distribution networks, we have opportunities to match your interests.

Available Opportunities

Farm Expansion

Invest in our land acquisition and cultivation expansion project to increase production capacity by 300% over the next 3 years.

Investment Range: $50,000 - $500,000

ROI Projection: 18-25% annually

Processing Unit

Partner in establishing a modern rice processing plant with sorting, milling, packaging, and storage facilities.

Investment Range: $200,000 - $1,000,000

ROI Projection: 20-30% annually

Distribution Network

Help develop our nationwide distribution channels and export capabilities to neighboring countries.

Investment Range: $30,000 - $300,000

ROI Projection: 15-22% annually

Partner Benefits

  • Attractive return on investment in a growing market
  • Government tax incentives for agricultural investments
  • Regular financial reporting and transparency
  • Opportunity to visit the farm and see operations firsthand
  • Social impact through job creation and community development
