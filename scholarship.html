Buhlami Educational Scholarship Program

Investing in the future of our community through education

Program Overview

The Buhlami Educational Scholarship Program was established in 2020 as part of our corporate social responsibility initiatives. Recognizing that education is the foundation for sustainable development, we commit 5% of our annual profits to support bright but financially disadvantaged students from farming communities in the Ndop area.

Our scholarship program focuses on students pursuing agricultural sciences and related fields, though we also support exceptional students in other disciplines. The goal is to develop skilled professionals who can contribute to Cameroon's agricultural transformation while giving back to their communities.

Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for the Buhlami Scholarship, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Be a resident of Ngoketunjia Division or surrounding areas
  • Come from a farming family with demonstrated financial need
  • Maintain excellent academic performance (minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 or equivalent)
  • For university students: pursuing studies in agriculture, agribusiness, or related fields
  • Demonstrate leadership potential and commitment to community development

Application Process

1

Application Submission

Complete the online application form and submit required documents including academic transcripts, recommendation letters, and personal statement.

2

Initial Screening

Our committee reviews all applications to verify eligibility and academic qualifications.

3

Interviews

Shortlisted candidates are invited for interviews with the selection committee.

4

Home Visits

For finalists, we conduct home visits to verify family circumstances.

5

Award Notification

Successful applicants are notified and award ceremonies are held.
