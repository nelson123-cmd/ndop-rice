Meet Our Visionary Founder

SENGLA NELSON

Founder & CEO, Buhlami Enterprise

Against all odds, Sengla Nelson has transformed from a university dropout to one of Cameroon's most promising agricultural entrepreneurs. Coming from a humble background in Ndop, Nelson's journey is a testament to the power of determination and hands-on learning.

With two years of formal training in agrofarming and countless more in the fields, Nelson combines technical knowledge with practical experience. His unique perspective allows Buhlami Enterprise to bridge traditional farming wisdom with modern agricultural techniques.

"I dropped out of university not because I couldn't learn, but because I wanted to learn differently - directly from the soil that feeds us," Nelson often says. This philosophy drives Buhlami's commitment to practical, sustainable agriculture that benefits both farmers and consumers.

Under Nelson's leadership, Buhlami has grown from a small family plot to a thriving enterprise employing over 100 local community members while maintaining its commitment to quality and sustainability.
